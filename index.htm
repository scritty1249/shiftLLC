Welcome to Shift-In LLC

What is Shift-In?

    At Shift-In LLC, our mission statement is to bring back the joys of driving and owning a manual stick-shift vehicle by bringing our lessons to the local car and automotive communities. By reintroducing manual clutch driving to the regular automatic-driving enthusiasts, we aim to open up our drivers' selection to other affordable and vintage vehicles when car-shopping while also deepening their connection to the community with strengthened driving knowledge.

Meet Shift-In LLC

    Founded in April 2023, by member David Gibson, as a young business owner, he has always found passion in working in close proximity with the car community from washing cars, to modding his very own manual Acura TL. In doing so, he wanted to use his experience and knowledge to bring the joy he felt driving a manual clutch vehicle to the average driver or other car-enthusiasts with Shift-In LLC, a growing, community-based manual driving school.
