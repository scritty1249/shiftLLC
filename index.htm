What is Shift-In?

    At Shift-In LLC, our mission statement is to bring back the joys of driving and owning a manual stick-shift vehicle by bringing our lessons to the local car and automotive communities. By reintroducing manual clutch driving to the regular automatic-driving enthusiasts, we aim to open up our drivers' selection to other affordable and vintage vehicles when car-shopping while also deepening their connection to the community with strengthened driving knowledge.
