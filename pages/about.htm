David.

Founder

Founded in April 2023, by member David Gibson, as a young business owner, he has always found passion in working in close proximity with the car community from washing cars, to modding his very own manual Acura TL. In doing so, he wanted to use his experience and knowledge to bring the joy he felt driving a manual clutch vehicle to the average driver or other car-enthusiasts with Shift-In LLC, a growing, community-based manual driving school.
